FAQ

Common questions from property and facility teams.

Request placement
Does it cost the venue to host a machine?

Typically, no. We review placement fit and operating terms during the placement review process.

What do you need from the venue?

A visible placement area, standard power nearby, and reasonable access for restocking/service.

Can we request specific products?

Yes. Share brand/flavor/size preferences. We curate based on availability and venue fit.

How do you handle service and restocking?

We restock on a routine schedule and provide support to keep the setup clean, reliable, and consistent.
