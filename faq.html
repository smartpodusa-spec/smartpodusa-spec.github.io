FAQ

Answers for property and facility teams evaluating SmartPod as an amenity.

What is SmartPod?

SmartPod is a fully managed on-site vending amenity. We install the unit, stock consumable products, and provide ongoing service.

Do you sell vending machines to operators?

No. SmartPod is designed for locations that want the amenity. We are not selling machines or a “vending business opportunity.”

What does a location need to provide?

A high-visibility placement area, nearby power access, and reasonable access for restock/service visits. A point-of-contact helps coordination.

How do you decide if a location qualifies?

We review venue type, estimated daily traffic, visibility, placement area, and service coverage. We may ask for a photo of the proposed placement area.

How often do you restock?

Restocking depends on demand and access. We aim for a predictable cadence and adjust based on product movement and availability.

Who handles maintenance and service?

SmartPod coordinates maintenance and support. If an on-site issue occurs, we work with your point-of-contact to schedule service access.

Can we request certain products?

Yes. Use the Products page to submit preferences (brand + flavor + size helps). We evaluate fit based on demand and availability.

How long does install take after approval?

Timing depends on scheduling and site readiness. After approval, we coordinate delivery and installation steps with your team.
