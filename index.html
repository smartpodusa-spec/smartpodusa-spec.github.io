FAQ

Common questions from property managers and operators.

What does it cost the location?

For qualified locations, placement is typically zero upfront cost. Terms may vary by location type, traffic, and placement area.

Do you offer commission or revenue share?

Some locations qualify for a commission structure based on volume and placement. We confirm options during qualification.

What do you need from the location?

Generally: a suitable footprint, standard power access, and reasonable access for stocking and service.

Can we request specific products?

Yes. We aim to match your audience and can adjust the mix over time based on demand and availability.

How quickly can you install?

Timeline depends on qualification, site readiness, and machine availability. We provide an estimated schedule after review.
