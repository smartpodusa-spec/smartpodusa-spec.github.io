FAQ

Common questions from property managers and operators.

Does it cost us anything to host a machine?

Many qualified locations have no upfront cost. Terms depend on traffic, placement area, and service coverage.

Who stocks and maintains the machine?

SmartPod stocks, services, and maintains the machine. We coordinate access for routine visits.

Can we request specific products?

Yes. We can start with your preferences and adjust inventory based on sales and availability.

How fast can we install?

After qualification and site review, we provide an estimated timeline based on readiness and availability.
