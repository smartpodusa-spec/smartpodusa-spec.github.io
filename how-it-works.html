Process overview

Step 1

Placement request

Venue type, city/state, estimated daily traffic, and preferred placement area.

Step 2

Qualification review

We confirm fit based on visibility, traffic patterns, power access, and service coverage.

Step 3

Site readiness check

Footprint, power outlet location, ADA-safe clearance, access for restock/service visits.

Step 4

Install + launch

Delivery, placement, initial stock, and operational validation.

Step 5

Ongoing operations

Restock cadence + maintenance response + product optimization over time.

Responsibilities

What the venue provides

  • Approved placement area (high visibility)
  • Standard power access
  • Reasonable access for service visits
  • Point-of-contact for scheduling (preferred)

What SmartPod handles

  • Installation and initial stocking
  • Inventory management and restocking
  • Maintenance coordination and support
  • Product mix updates based on demand

Site readiness checklist

Use this to pre-check a location before requesting placement.

Visibility

High-traffic line of sight (lobby, breakroom, gym floor, waiting area).

Power + footprint

Accessible outlet near the placement area and adequate floor space.

Service access

Predictable access for restock and maintenance without disrupting operations.
